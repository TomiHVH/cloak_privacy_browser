What is WebGL Fingerprinting?

WebGL fingerprinting is a tracking technique where websites can identify your browser based on your GPU information. This includes:

  • Vendor: Your GPU manufacturer (Intel, NVIDIA, AMD, etc.)
  • Renderer: Your specific GPU model
  • Version: Your WebGL/OpenGL version

This information can be used to create a unique "fingerprint" that tracks you across websites, even when you clear cookies or use incognito mode.
